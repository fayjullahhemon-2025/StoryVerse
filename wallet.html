Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to unlock a chapter?

Chapter prices vary: Standard chapters cost ৳10, Special chapters cost ৳15, and Finale chapters cost ৳20. The first 3-5 chapters are usually free.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes! We use secure payment gateways (bKash, Rocket, Nagad) that are certified and trusted by millions of users in Bangladesh. We never store your PIN or sensitive payment information.

Can I get a refund after unlocking a chapter?

Once a chapter is unlocked, it remains accessible to you permanently. Refunds are only available in case of technical errors or duplicate payments.

Do I need to pay again to re-read a chapter?

No! Once you unlock a chapter, you can read it unlimited times without paying again. It's yours forever.

What if my payment fails?

If your payment fails, please check your account balance and try again. If the problem persists, contact your payment provider's customer service or reach out to our support team.

Can I use multiple payment methods?

Yes! You can use any of our supported payment methods (bKash, Rocket, Nagad) for different transactions. Choose whichever is most convenient for you.
