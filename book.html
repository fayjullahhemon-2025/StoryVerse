Synopsis

In a world where magic has faded and kingdoms crumble, one young warrior must rise to reclaim her birthright. Princess Aria thought she was just another orphan until the day she discovered the ancient sword that only the true heir to the throne could wield.

Now hunted by dark forces and betrayed by those she trusted, Aria must gather allies from the most unlikely places. With the help of a mysterious mage, a reformed thief, and a dragon thought to be extinct, she embarks on a quest to restore the Last Kingdom before it's lost forever.

But the greatest enemy might not be the dark lord threatening her kingdom—it might be the secrets of her own past.
